Why do YouTube videos freeze at 301 views?

Creator: Numberphile

Upload date: June 22nd 2012

Video resolution: 1280x720 (720p)

Language: English

Highlighted description quote: "This glitch was finally "fixed" in August 2015, but may it live on in our hearts."

View other video information

File size: 63,645,415 bytes (63,645.41 Kilobytes) (63.64 Megabytes)

File format: MP4

Duration: 9 minutes 47 seconds (587 seconds)

Framerate: 25 frames per second

Download link:

Significance:

This video talks about an early YouTube glitch that was fixed in August 2015 when YouTube videos freeze at 301 views to verify the view count. However, YouTube also did something special for this video: they locked the view count at 301

PSY - GANGNAM STYLE(강남스타일) M/V

Creator: Psy

Upload date: July 15th 2012

Video resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)

Language: Korean

View other video information

File size: 86,211,090 bytes (86,211.09 Kilobytes) (86.21 Megabytes)

File format: MP4 (MPEG Part 14)

Duration: 4 Minute 12 seconds (252 seconds)

Framerate: 24 frames per second

Download link:

Significance:

This was the first video to exceed the maximum value of a 32 bit integer in views, and YouTube views were still being recorded by a 32 bit integer. This video raised the roof and forced YouTube to switch the view counter to a 64 bit integer. It was also the first video to reach 1 billion views
